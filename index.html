HealthFace

Better personal Health insurance through technology

PHR

An online portable health record containing lifestyle, nutrition and diagnostics records. Data sharing controlled through strict consent of the owner. Benefits of such a system is tremendous and will move our health systems from being data-poor to data-rich.

Symptom Diagnosis

A chat-bot like interface for symptom diagnosis built over a knowledge graph derived from anonymised population data from the PHR database. The diagnosis can be used in patient screening, education and recommending follow-up actions for non-critical conditions.

Customer segmentation

Individuals segmented by health profiles derived from clustering anonymised PHR population data. Create highly personalised products targeting specific segments e.g. Health Insurance. Rewards and Feedback system incentivises individual towards healthier lifestyle.
